Frequently Asked Questions
How can I inquire about traffic fines? +

You can easily inquire by selecting the appropriate service (Plate, License, or Traffic File) and entering the required details.

Is the payment portal safe and secure? +

Yes, we use high-level SSL encryption and certified payment gateways to ensure your financial data is fully protected.

When will the fine be updated after payment? +

Fines are usually updated in the official system within a few minutes up to 24 working hours after a successful transaction.

Can I pay fines for all UAE Emirates? +

Yes, our platform supports inquiry and payment services for traffic fines registered across all Emirates of the UAE.

What should I do if the fine still appears after payment? +

Please wait for the system to sync. If it persists after 24 hours, contact our technical support with your transaction receipt.

Are there any additional service fees? +

A small administrative fee may apply for providing fast electronic collection services and technical facilitation.

How do I receive my payment receipt? +

A digital receipt will be sent automatically to your registered email address immediately after the payment is confirmed.

Can I dispute a fine through this website? +

No, this platform is for inquiry and payment only. Disputes must be filed through the official channels of the respective traffic authorities.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and local cards authorized in the UAE.

Is the service available during holidays? +

Yes, the platform is available 24/7, allowing you to inquire and pay for fines at any time, including weekends and holidays.
